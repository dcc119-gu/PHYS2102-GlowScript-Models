Compilation of projects from computational modeling for Physics 2102: Electromagnetic Phenomena. Descriptions of each lab are as follows: <br />
1. Calculated and visualized electric field from single electron at 12 observation points. <br />
2. Calculated and visualized electric field due to "real" dipole and compared to calculation from equation of approximation. <br />
3. Calculated and visualized electric field due to charged rod at several observation points varying in distance and angle from the rod. <br />
4. Computed integral of capacitance from measurements taken in real life and compared it to the value given by real equation. <br />
5. Modeled & visualized magnetic field at 6 observation points on y-z plane due to proton moving along x-axis.
